Before Sunrise

Dir. Richard Linklater - 1995

Summary:

"Can the greatest romance of your life last only one night? A young man and woman meet on a train in Europe, and wind up spending one evening together in Vienna. Unfortunately, both know that this will probably be their only night together."


Review:

The greatest romance movie ever made. If you are one of those people that romanticize Europe then this is the movie for you. Instantly became one of my favourite movies after my first watch. Ethan Hawke and Julie Delpy are amazing in this movie and they bring so much charisma to the dialogue.

10/10
